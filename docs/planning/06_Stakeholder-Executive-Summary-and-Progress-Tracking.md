# Handwerker OnDemand Stakeholder Executive Summary and Progress Tracking

## 1. Executive Summary

Handwerker OnDemand is a regional service marketplace designed to connect customers with verified tradespeople for urgent and scheduled jobs. The strongest opportunity is not a broad national rollout on day one, but a focused pilot with enough local supply density, strong trust controls, and measurable unit economics.

## 2. Recommended Strategic Positioning

- start with a narrow launch region
- focus on a limited set of trade categories with repeat demand
- build trust through verification, transparent pricing models, and reliable support
- prove provider response times and repeat usage before geographic expansion

## 3. Major Delivery Decisions

- architecture: cloud-native, domain-oriented service platform
- launch scope: pilot region first, not nationwide
- product scope: one shared product app launched on web, iOS, and Android for MVP
- commercial scope: hybrid urgent + scheduled jobs
- financial scope: use compliant marketplace payment/payout vendor

### Platform strategy decision

- treat delivery as cross-platform-first, not separate-platform-first
- keep UX mobile-first because the dominant usage is expected on phones
- use one shared codebase for the customer/provider product app wherever practical
- keep internal admin and support tooling as a separate web-first surface

## 4. What Stakeholders Should Expect

### By milestone M2

- a demonstrable end-to-end booking flow
- that booking flow available on web, iOS, and Android for pilot scope
- verified provider onboarding
- basic analytics and operations visibility

### By milestone M4

- controlled external pilot readiness
- payment and payout flow in staging/production-like conditions
- support and incident playbooks ready

### By milestone M5

- evidence for go/no-go on regional expansion
- initial unit economics and retention trends

## 5. KPI Framework

### Marketplace supply KPIs

- verified providers onboarded
- active providers accepting jobs
- median provider response time
- provider acceptance rate

### Demand KPIs

- job requests created
- booking conversion rate
- repeat booking rate
- average time-to-first-acceptance

### Quality KPIs

- job completion rate
- cancellation rate
- dispute rate
- average rating

### Financial KPIs

- gross merchandise volume
- net revenue / take rate
- payment success rate
- payout delay rate

### Operating KPIs

- support tickets per 100 bookings
- incident count by severity
- SLA attainment
